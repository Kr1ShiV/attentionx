# 🎬 AttentionX — Demo Presentation Script

## Pre-Demo Setup
1. Start the backend: `python backend/main.py`
2. Open browser: http://localhost:8000
3. Have a sample video ready (60-120 seconds works best)
4. Practice the flow 2-3 times

---

## Demo Flow (5 Minutes)

### 🎙️ [0:00 - 0:30] THE HOOK (30 seconds)

> "Imagine you're a creator or educator who just recorded a 60-minute lecture. 
> Somewhere in that video are 3-4 moments that could go VIRAL. 
> But finding them manually takes hours.
>
> What if an AI could predict which moments will go viral, 
> generate scroll-stopping hooks, and optimize for every platform 
> — all in under a minute?
>
> That's AttentionX."

**[Show the landing page with the beautiful dark UI]**

---

### 📤 [0:30 - 1:30] UPLOAD & PROCESSING (60 seconds)

**Option A: Upload a real video**
1. Drag & drop a video file onto the upload zone
2. Show the processing pipeline:
   - "Watch as the AI extracts audio..."
   - "Now it's transcribing with Whisper..."
   - "Analyzing audio patterns with Librosa..."
   - "Tracking faces with MediaPipe..."
   - "And now... predicting viral moments"

**Option B: Use Demo Mode (RECOMMENDED for reliability)**
1. Click "🎮 Try Demo" button
2. Say: "Let me load our pre-analyzed demo — a startup masterclass"
3. Dashboard loads instantly

---

### 📊 [1:30 - 2:30] DASHBOARD — THE WOW MOMENT (60 seconds)

**Stats Bar:**
> "The AI found 9 segments, with an average virality score of 62. 
> But look — 4 segments scored above 70. These are your golden moments."

**Attention Heatmap:**
> "This is our Attention Heatmap. Red zones are viral gold — 
> the moments your audience will love. Blue zones? That's where they'd scroll away."

**[Hover over the heatmap to show interactive tooltips]**

**Audio Analysis:**
> "We're analyzing energy, pitch, speech rate, and intensity. 
> Watch how the energy spikes perfectly align with the high-virality zones."

**[Toggle between Energy, Pitch, Intensity, Speech Rate]**

---

### 🎬 [2:30 - 3:30] CLIPS & HOOKS (60 seconds)

**Navigate to Clips tab**

> "Each segment is scored. This one — 92 out of 100 — 
> look why: 'High emotional intensity, peak audio energy, 
> and it contains the viral keyword secret.'"

**Click on a high-scoring clip card**

> "Now watch this — our Hook Generator creates 3 scroll-stopping hooks. 
> I can switch between styles: Curiosity, Shock, Challenge, Story, Value."

**[Click different hook styles to regenerate]**

> "Each hook is copy-paste ready. One click to copy."

**[Click copy to show the clipboard toast]**

---

### 🔥 [3:30 - 4:30] REMIX MODE — THE KILLER FEATURE (60 seconds)

**Navigate to Remix tab**

**Persona Adapter:**
> "Here's what makes us unique. Same content, 5 different audiences. 
> Let's adapt this clip for Developers..."

**[Click Developers persona card]**

> "The AI completely rewrites the tone, creates developer-specific hooks, 
> and even adds relevant hashtags."

**[Click Entrepreneurs to show the contrast]**

> "Now for Entrepreneurs — completely different tone, action-oriented, ROI-focused."

**Platform Remix:**
> "But we go further. Content Remix Mode — same clip, optimized for 
> Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, AND TikTok."

**[Click Instagram Reel]**

> "Each platform gets custom captions, hashtags, format guidelines, 
> and call-to-action. This is what real-world creators need."

**[Click LinkedIn to show the text-based storytelling format]**

> "This is not just a clipper. This is a Content Intelligence Engine.

## 🎯 Key Points to Emphasize

1. **"Not just a clipper"** — Repeat this phrase. We predict, adapt, and optimize.
2. **Virality Score** — Show the numbers. Data-driven decisions.
3. **One clip → 4 platforms** — The Remix Mode is the WOW moment.
4. **Story Builder** — "No other hackathon team has this."
5. **Beautiful UI** — Let the design speak. Don't rush past it.

## ⚠️ Backup Plans

- **If upload takes too long:** Use Demo Mode immediately
- **If API fails:** The frontend has complete client-side demo data as fallback
- **If hooks don't generate:** Pattern-based hooks still work without API
- **If someone asks about scalability:** "We've designed modular services — 
  each can be independently scaled. The virality engine is stateless."
